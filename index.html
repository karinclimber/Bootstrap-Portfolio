About Me

I am a rock climbing barista and pet sitter living in Davidson, NC. I enjoy spending time with my boyfriend (Michael) and my dog (Melly) in the mountains. I also enjoy paleo cooking, nutrition, making earrings, and playing Two Dots and Tetris.

I decided to learn coding a few month ago when a friend pointed out the similarities between coding and rock climbing. Problem solving, constant challenges, brief victories, and unlimited amounts of frustration. I looked for a bootcamp that would provide a full-stack education to learn as much as I could and settled on the UNCC program. I am very excited about this journey.
